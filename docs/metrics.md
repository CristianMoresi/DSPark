- **THD+N**: spectral energy outside the fundamental, 1 kHz tone at -6 dBFS,
  relative to the fundamental, 20 Hz-20 kHz.
- **Noise floor**: output RMS (dBFS) with silent input.

  10.1 kHz tone at -6 dBFS — aliasing shows up here.
- **Latency**: as reported by `getLatency()` (samples @ 48 kHz). Validated
  by the PDC null tests in this same suite.
